Chapter 3: The Threat to Bananas

Section 1: Modern Days

In the modern day, bananas face a serious threat from a fungal disease called Panama disease. This disease has wiped out entire banana plantations in the past and continues to pose a risk to banana crops worldwide. Scientists are working hard to develop resistant banana varieties to ensure that this beloved fruit remains available for future generations.

Section 2: The Future Banana

In the future, we may see genetically modified bananas that are resistant to diseases and pests. These bananas could help ensure a stable supply of this popular fruit for years to come. Additionally, researchers are exploring new ways to grow bananas in different climates and environments, which could help expand banana production to new regions.
